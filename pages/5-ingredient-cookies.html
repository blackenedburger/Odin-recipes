5-Ingredient Peanut Butter Chocolate Cookies

These cookies are simple, yet effective, at containing that urge for a peanuty chocolate taste. It is an easy and quick recipe that leaves very little clean up. It is an excellent idea for a free afternoon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 egg
  • Â¼ cup stevia
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine peanut butter, egg, stevia sweetener, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract together with a mixer or food processor. Roll dough into 1-inch balls. Place 1 to 2 inches apart on an ungreased baking sheet. Flatten balls with a fork.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set, about 10 minutes. Let cool on the baking sheet for 10 minutes.
  4. Line a tray with waxed paper. Transfer cookies to tray until cooled to room temperature, about 15 minutes, then refrigerate for 30 minutes
